【实习】【产品】【微软】产品经理实习生

发表于2023-06-04|更新于2023-06-04|实习产品

微软Bing news team聘PM实习生，要求实习期4个月以上，不提供转正机会有意向的同学请将中英文简历请发送至ruozeng@microsoft.com 邮件命名格式：【PM实习生】+【姓名】+【毕业时间】+【学校】+【年级】+【可实习时长】
JD: Work with cross-discipline designer, PM and dev teams to deliver first-class news search and feed product of high quality and outstanding user experience for global users. You will be a sharp user speaker, a creative thinker, a project driver, and a problem solver.
What we value -1. Proficiency at English reading (as the daily routine would include and heavily rely on the capability to read news in English), Fluent written and spoken English2. Strong responsibility and sense of ownership - Be self-driven and proactive to drive the project and push forward the progress. Self motivation to pursue efficiency and perfection.3. Ability to work with designer and dev peers to develop, test and deliver features4. Fast learning skill and the willingness to learn, explore, and dissolve the ambiguities when faced with problems and challenges5. Customer obsession - you should be customer obsession and help team to delivery products with best user experience6. What’s more: We are delivering products for global users, candidates with multi-lingual skills will get opportunity to practice:)
